Create a CRM and operations base for Royal Fit Uniform - a B2B uniform company in India selling to hotels and hospitals.

TABLES NEEDED:
1. Leads - Customer inquiries from website
2. Products - Uniform catalog
3. Orders - Confirmed orders
4. Analytics - Business metrics

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
TABLE 1: LEADS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Track customer quote requests with these fields:

CUSTOMER INFO:
- Company Name (text)
- Company Type (single select: Hotel, Hospital, Other)
- Facility Size (single select: Small, Medium, Large, Enterprise)
- Contact Name (text)
- Job Title (text)
- Email (email)
- Phone (phone number)

REQUIREMENTS:
- Departments (multiple select: Front of House, Kitchen & Service, Housekeeping, Management, Nursing & Care, Surgical & Specialist, Admin & Support, Management & Leadership)
- Total Quantity (single select: Under 50, 50-100, 100-200, 200-500, 500+)
- Logo Customization (checkbox)
- Embroidery (checkbox)
- Color Match (checkbox)

SALES PIPELINE:
- Status (single select: New, Contacted, Qualified, Proposal Sent, Won, Lost)
- Priority (single select: High, Medium, Low)
- Timeline (single select: Urgent, Planned, Exploratory)
- Budget Range (single select: Under 1L, 1-5L, 5-10L, 10L+)
- Estimated Value (currency INR)

TRACKING:
- Source (single select: Website, Phone, Email, Referral, Trade Show)
- Assigned To (text)
- Submitted At (date with time)
- Follow Up Date (date)
- Notes (long text)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
TABLE 2: PRODUCTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Manage uniform catalog:

BASIC INFO:
- Product ID (text)
- Name (text)
- Category (single select: Hotel, Hospital)
- Department (single select: Front of House, Kitchen & Service, Housekeeping, Management, Nursing & Care, Surgical & Specialist, Admin & Support, Management & Leadership)
- Description (long text)

SPECIFICATIONS:
- Fabric (text)
- Features (long text)
- Durability Rating (number 0-5, 1 decimal)
- Sizes (multiple select: XS, S, M, L, XL, XXL, XXXL)
- Colors (multiple select: Navy Blue, White, Black, Ceil Blue, Surgical Green, etc.)

PRICING & STOCK:
- Price Per Unit (currency INR)
- Customizable (checkbox)
- In Stock (checkbox)
- Stock Quantity (number)
- Minimum Order Qty (number)
- Lead Time Days (number)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Track confirmed orders:

ORDER INFO:
- Order ID (autonumber with prefix "ORD-")

- Company Name (text)
- Order Date (date with time)

ORDER DETAILS:
- Products Ordered (long text)
- Total Items (number)
- Subtotal (currency INR)
- Discount % (number 0-100)
- Tax Amount (currency INR)
- Total Amount (currency INR)

STATUS TRACKING:
- Status (single select: Draft, Confirmed, In Production, Quality Check, Shipped, Delivered, Cancelled)
- Payment Status (single select: Pending, Partial, Paid, Refunded)
- Payment Terms (single select: 30% Advance, 50% Advance, 100% Advance, Net 30, Net 60)

FULFILLMENT:
- Expected Delivery (date)
- Actual Delivery (date)
- Shipping Address (long text)
- Tracking Number (text)
- Notes (long text)

- Revenue (currency INR)
